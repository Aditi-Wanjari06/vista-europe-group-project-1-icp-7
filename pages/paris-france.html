Paris the City Of Lights

Paris, France

Paris, the capital city of France, is globally celebrated for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine, and is renowned for landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, the Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum. Known as the "City of Light" (La Ville Lumière) for its role in the Age of Enlightenment and for its literally dazzling illumination, Paris is a dream destination for travelers worldwide.
